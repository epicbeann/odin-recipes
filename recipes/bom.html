Blood Orange Mimosa

A properly made Blood Orange Mimosa

This is a great blood orange-pineapple mimosa for any lazy morning.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup pink sanding sugar
  • 1 bottle brut champagne
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup blood orange juice
  • 2 fluid ounces orange liquer

Steps

  1. Dip the rims of 4 champagne flutes in water. Dip into sanding sugar to coat the rims.
  2. Divide Champagne among the flutes. Pour 2 tablespoons pineapple juice and 2 tablespoons blood orange juice into each. Top mimosas with orange liquer
