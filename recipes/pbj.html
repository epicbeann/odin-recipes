Peanut Butter and Jelly

A unique twist on a classic

A double decker PB&J that is very filling and awesome!

Ingredients

  • 3 slices bread
  • 2 tablespoons fruit preserves, any flavor
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Steps

  1. Toast 1 bread slice, allow to cool.
  2. Spread one side of each of the two remaining slices with preserves.
  3. Spread both sides of the toasted slice with peanut butter.
  4. Form a sandwich with the toasted slice in the center.
